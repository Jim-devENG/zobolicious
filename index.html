we're zobolicious

Since we started doing business back in 2020, we've been committed to doing it in the right way - standing up for what matters and leaving things better than we find them. We've been a proud B Corp since 2022, meaning we're officially using business as a force for good. And we put people and the planet at the heart of everything we do, like giving 10% of our profits to good causes and building our sustainable drinks factory, the Zobolender.
