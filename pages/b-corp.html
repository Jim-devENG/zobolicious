what is b corp?

B Corp certification is a rigorous standard that measures a company's entire social and environmental impact. It's not just about what we do, but how we do it.

To become certified, we had to demonstrate high standards of verified performance, accountability, and transparency across five key areas: governance, workers, community, environment, and customers.

governance

How we make decisions and ensure accountability.

workers

How we treat our employees and create a positive workplace.

community

How we contribute to our local and global communities.

environment

How we minimize our environmental impact.

customers

How we create value for our customers.
