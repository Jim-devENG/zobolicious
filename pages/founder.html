sarah johnson

Sarah founded Zobolicious in 2020 with a simple but powerful vision: to create delicious, healthy drinks that don't compromise on taste, ethics, or the environment.

With over 15 years of experience in the food and beverage industry, Sarah has always believed that business can and should be a force for good. Her passion for sustainability, social justice, and creating products that people actually want to consume has been the driving force behind Zobolicious's success.

"we're not just making drinks, we're making a difference"

This isn't just a tagline for us - it's our guiding principle. Every decision we make, from sourcing ingredients to packaging choices, is driven by our commitment to people and planet.
